Privacy Policy — Catholic Calendar

Last updated: April 1, 2026

Catholic Calendar ("the app") is developed by Vice Madunic.

INFORMATION WE COLLECT

The app does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data to external servers.

CONTACTS

The app optionally accesses your device's contacts solely to match first names against Catholic name days. This processing happens entirely on your device. Contact data is never read beyond first names, never stored by the app, and never transmitted anywhere.

DATA STORAGE

All app preferences (language, appearance) are stored locally on your device using iOS standard storage (UserDefaults). No data leaves your device.

THIRD PARTY SERVICES

The app does not use any third-party analytics, advertising networks, or tracking services.

CHILDREN'S PRIVACY

he app does not knowingly collect any information from children under 13.

CHANGES TO THIS POLICY

Any future changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date.
